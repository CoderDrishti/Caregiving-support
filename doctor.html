Types of Doctors

Specializations

General Practitioner (GP) Provide comprehensive and continuous care tailored to the unique needs of elderly patients.
Manage multiple chronic conditions, ensuring coordinated and holistic healthcare.

Geriatrician Focus on promoting independence, mobility, and overall well-being in elderly patients through personalized care plans.
Provide specialized medical care tailored to the unique needs of old people.

Cardiologist Provide specialized care for heart-related conditions like hypertension, heart failure, and arrhythmias.
Monitor and manage cardiovascular health to prevent serious complications and enhance longevity.

Dermatologist Address skin issues related to aging, such as dryness, skin cancer, and age spots.
Provide treatments and preventive care to maintain healthy skin and manage chronic skin conditions.

Endocrinologist Treat and manage diabetes, helping to control blood sugar levels and prevent complications.
Diagnose and treat thyroid conditions, ensuring balanced hormone levels and improved overall health.

Gastroenterologist Diagnose and treat digestive disorders such as acid reflux, gastritis, and diverticulitis.
Improving gastrointestinal health and comfort.

Neurologist Diagnose and treat neurological disorders such as Alzheimer’s, Parkinson’s, and strokes, which are common in the elderly.
Offer therapies and management strategies to maintain cognitive function and quality of life.

Oncologist Offer specialized cancer care, including advanced treatments and support, tailored to the needs of elderly patients.
Provide comprehensive care plans that focus on both treatment and quality of life.

ENT Specialist Treat age-related hearing loss and prescribe hearing aids, improving communication.
Address balance disorders like vertigo, reducing the risk of falls and injuries.

Pulmonologist Diagnose and treat chronic conditions like COPD and asthma, improving breathing and quality of life.
Manage sleep apnea and other sleep-related disorders, enhancing sleep quality and overall health.

Rheumatologist reat arthritis to reduce pain and improve joint mobility.
Manage conditions like lupus, offering symptom relief and better quality of life.
